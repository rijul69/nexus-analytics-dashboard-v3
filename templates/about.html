Nexus Analytics

Nexus Analytics is a full-stack, business-intelligence-grade e-commerce sales analytics platform built as a Data Science major project. It transforms a raw transactional e-commerce dataset into a real-time, interactive executive dashboard — covering sales, customers, payments, reviews and seller performance — with the look and feel of production BI tools like Power BI, Tableau and Amazon Seller Central.

Technology Stack

Python Flask Pandas Plotly Bootstrap 5 HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript

Key Features

Executive Dashboard

8 real-time KPIs and 12 interactive Plotly charts summarizing sales, orders and fulfillment health.

Dynamic Filtering

Filter every page live by year, month, state, payment type and category — all charts and KPIs recompute instantly.

360° Customer View

Geography, top spenders, city-level breakdowns and loyalty metrics on a dedicated customer page.

Product Intelligence

Category performance table, top products, and pricing analytics computed directly from the dataset.

Review Sentiment

Rating distribution, category satisfaction rankings and rating trend over time.

Seller Leaderboard

Top-performing sellers by revenue, state-level seller density, and full leaderboard table.

The dataset (cleaned_ecommerce.csv) contains 42 columns covering order lifecycle timestamps, customer and seller geography, product attributes, payment details, review scores and derived fields such as Delivery_Days and Total_Amount. It spans multiple years of e-commerce transactions and powers every metric on this dashboard directly — no static or placeholder values are used.
